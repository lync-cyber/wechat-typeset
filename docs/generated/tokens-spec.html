§ 01

Color

Three materials hold the system: paper (warm neutral base), ink (warm black), and proof red. Three semantic hues (sage · amber · indigo-ink) exist so that states read as states, not as brand. Proof red is allowed in exactly three places: primary action, errors, and brand/ruler marks.

§ 05

Shadow

Three tiers. Shadows are tinted warm-ink rgba(35, 30, 20, α) rather than neutral black — the "paper" base calls for it. One focus ring, double-stroke, pausing proof red at 45 % alpha.

Shadow · Rest
Toolbar底边、input 静态态。几乎察觉不到，仅给出"纸压着纸"的提示。
Shadow · Lift
抽屉、浮动面板。投影方向略偏下，暗示"自上而下"照明。
Shadow · Modal
存选对话框、确认。最重的一档——"校样置于工作台"的意象。
Focus · Ring
双层外环：先 2 px 底色、再 2 px 朱红半透明。键盘可达性优先。
§ 06

Motion

One easing curve — cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.7, 0.1, 1) — three durations. No spring, no bounce, no overshoot. Hover each card to run the curve; reduced-motion users receive 0 ms.

§ 07

Icons

16 / 24 px grid. 1.25 px stroke, square caps, miter joins, single currentColor. Filled forms reserved for indicators (status dots, numeric badges). Never used as a substitute for a text label.
